# 📌 Project Background

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built using the **Adidas US Sales dataset**. The dashboard transforms raw sales transactions into meaningful business insights by analyzing revenue, profitability, product performance, regional sales, customer purchasing trends, and sales channels.

The objective is to provide an executive-level Business Intelligence solution that enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify business opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This dashboard aims to answer the following business questions:

- How much revenue and operating profit has Adidas generated?
- Which cities contribute the highest sales?
- Which product categories perform the best?
- Which sales channel generates the highest revenue?
- How are sales distributed across different regions?
- How has sales performance changed throughout the year?
- Which retailers contribute most to overall business performance?

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

## 📈 Overall Business Performance

- Adidas generated **$77.70 Million** in total sales revenue.
- Operating profit reached **$28.82 Million**, resulting in a healthy **41.79% average operating margin**.
- Nearly **198,000 units** were sold across all retailers and sales channels.

---

## 👟 Product Performance

- **Men's Street Footwear** emerged as the highest revenue-generating product category.
- **Men's Apparel** also contributed significantly to total sales.
- Women's product categories generated comparatively lower revenue, indicating opportunities for targeted marketing and product expansion.

---

## 🏙 Geographic Performance

- **Columbus** and **Manchester** generated the highest city-wise sales.
- The **Northeast** region recorded the highest overall revenue and profitability among all regions.
- Sales remained well distributed across multiple geographic markets, reducing dependence on a single region.

---

## 🛒 Sales Channel Performance

- **Online sales contributed 56.9%** of total revenue, making it the dominant sales channel.
- Outlet stores generated **30.7%** of total sales.
- In-store sales contributed **12.4%**, reflecting the growing importance of digital commerce.

---

## 📅 Sales Trend Analysis

- Sales demonstrated consistent growth throughout the year.
- Monthly revenue accelerated during the second half of the year.
- Peak sales occurred during **November and December**, highlighting strong holiday-season demand.
- Cumulative sales showed a steady upward trend throughout 2021.

---

## 📊 Profitability Analysis

- An average operating margin of **41.79%** indicates strong operational efficiency and consistent profitability.
- High-margin sales across leading products and regions contributed significantly to overall business performance.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Expand High-Performing Product Categories

Increase inventory allocation and marketing investment for **Men's Street Footwear** and **Men's Apparel**, which consistently generate the highest revenue.

### 2. Improve Women's Product Performance

Develop targeted promotional campaigns and product expansion strategies to increase sales of lower-performing women's product categories.

### 3. Strengthen Online Sales

With **56.9%** of total revenue coming from online sales, continued investment in e-commerce platforms, personalized recommendations, and digital marketing can further improve revenue growth.

### 4. Prioritize High-Revenue Cities

Focus inventory planning and localized marketing efforts on top-performing cities such as **Columbus** and **Manchester** to maximize sales opportunities.

### 5. Prepare for Seasonal Demand

Increase inventory and promotional campaigns before the **November–December** peak season to capitalize on higher customer demand.

### 6. Optimize Regional Strategy

Analyze customer behavior in lower-performing regions to identify opportunities for sales growth and improve regional market penetration.
